WHAT WE DO



We bring smile to lives & our planet

At Muskurate Raho, we work to bring smiles to lives and our planet by driving sustainable waste management through weekly beach cleanups, conducting plantation and restoration drives to save our saviors, and empowering underprivileged students through Project Pathshala’s transformative art and craft education initiatives.
